# ball-hitting-game

## Required Environment : 
### install 'pygame' module

## Ways of Execution :
### 1. Open command line and get into the directory of the files. Type 'python Menu_final.py' ,and then game surface will present.
### 2. Directly click on the 'Menu_final.py' file ,and then game surface will present.

## How to Play the Game :
### 1. Menu: There are two modes to choose from. To play, directly click on the button.
### 2. Preparation stage: Press SPACE to start the game.
### 3. Press UP(w),DOWN(s) to control the plate to hit the ball. Press LEFT(a) to rotate the plate counterclockwise, while RIGHT(d) to rotate it clockwise. UP,DOWN,LEFT,RIGHT are for player on the right side, while w,s,a,d for player on the left side.
### 4. The speed of the ball will grow faster and faster until it reaches a maximum limit as time goes by.
### 5. In 2player mode, if one player misses catching the ball, the other player will get one point, with the game going into next round. Winner of previous round can hit the ball first in present round. Each time player hits the ball, some energy is stored in the 'energy bar' shown on the upright(for right player) and the upleft(for left player). Once the energy accumulated reaches the maximum(hit the ball 5 times), player can press 'm' to launch smash ball skill.('m' is for right player, left player should press 'x')
### 6. In 1player mode, each time player hit the ball, some points will be added to the scoreboard on the upleft of the surface. The points you get is proportional to current ball speed. Once player missed the ball, one round is over. if the total score you get is the highest so far, it will be recorded as the 'highest score', which is shown every time in preparation stage. Click red 'X' to return to main menu, with your overall highest score shown for a few seconds.
### 7. In both modes, some treasure randomly shows on the surface. Player can hit the ball to touch them to get some special effects. The kinds and functions of all treasure are described in PowerPoint.
### 8. There are sound effects for the game, so be sure to turn on your speaker to attain better game-playing experience(?.
